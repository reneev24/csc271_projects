What is one+one?

One+one is a platform that allows users to make connections with anyone locally or internationally. It helps to simplify the problem of finding and connecting with others as it brings people right to the user. To quicken the process, one+one will put people that the user would more likely be interested first on their page.
One reason we're different from other platforms is our use of facial recongition. This will help prevent users from being catfished. This will make it harder for individuals to disguise themselves as other people.

Key Features of one+one

  • A Direct Message Page - this feature allows users to chat with people htat they've matched. It also shows current and past conversations they've had.
  • Personal Profile - here is where the user's photos and videos are, peronsal information, and friends/connections.
